FAQ

What makes a “verified proof” in VeriShot?

A VeriShot proof is a stamped photo that includes timestamp, GPS coordinates, a unique Proof ID, and a Verified status indicator.

Can I find proofs later?

Yes. Use the gallery to browse and search proofs by ID or date.

Can I see proofs on a map?

Yes. VeriShot can pin verifications by location so you can review geographically.

How do I share a proof?

Share the stamped image through Messages, email, AirDrop, or any share destination available on your device.

What devices are supported?

VeriShot supports iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 16+. iMac support is coming soon.
