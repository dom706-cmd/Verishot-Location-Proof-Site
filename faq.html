Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about VeriShot: location permissions, proof stamps, sharing, search, pricing, and device support.

Does VeriShot require location permission?

Yes. VeriShot uses your device’s location services to include GPS coordinates on the proof stamp. You can still take photos without location, but they may be marked unverified or won’t include coordinates, depending on your settings.

What’s included in the Verified Proof stamp?

A readable overlay with Proof ID, timestamp, latitude/longitude, and verification status—so recipients can understand the proof instantly.

Can I share proofs via Messages or Email?

Yes. Use the iOS share sheet to send a proof image through Messages, Mail, AirDrop, and other supported apps.

How do I find a proof later?

Open the Proof Gallery and search by Proof ID or date. Proof details also include the location with quick links to map apps.

Does VeriShot work on iPad?

Yes—VeriShot supports iPhone and iPad on iOS 16+.

Is there a Mac/iMac version?

An iMac version is coming soon. Today, you can view and share proofs from iPhone/iPad and AirDrop them to a Mac.

Can I remove the watermark or edit the stamp?

Proof stamps are designed to be clear and consistent. If your workflow needs a different stamp style or layout, contact support.

Do pricing and limits change?

Plan limits are shown in the app, and pricing is managed by Apple. You’ll always see the final price before purchase.
